About Us

To encourage young people to reach their full potential by providing quality services in a safe and supportive environment.

Our Philosophy

Gold Coast Project for Homeless Youth Inc. believes in:
• the right of people to make choices in their own lives.
• the right of people to dignity, respect, privacy and confidentiality.
• the right of people to be valued as individuals.
• the right of people to access services on a non-discriminatory basis.
• the right of the community to accountable and responsive services.

About GCPHY

Gold Coast Project for Homeless Youth Inc. (GCPHY) is an incorporated community organisation formed in 1984 to provide accommodation and support for homeless young people in the Gold Coast Local Government area.

GCPHY is a Specialist Homelessness Service and is funded by the Qld Government Department of Housing and Public Works.

GCPHY provides the following services:
• Short-Term supported accommodation.
• Medium-Term supported accommodation.
• Mobile Support Service.
• Referral to other agencies.
• Crisis Support.
• Advocacy.

Our goals are to:
• Reduce the cycle of homelessness.
• Provide a service which is quality assured.
• Offer a diversity of services with a holistic view to optimise client outcomes.
• Be adequately resourced to facilitate optimisation of client outcomes.
• Empower young people to lead safe and healthy lifestyles by making informed decisions.
