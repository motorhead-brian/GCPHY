Mission Statement

To encourage young people to reach their full potential by providing quality services in a safe and supportive environment.

WE ACCOMMODATE AND SUPPORT YOUNG PEOPLE WHO ARE:

• In crisis and in need of immediate accommodation (Lawson House).

• Disadvantaged or at risk and in need of transitional (medium term) accommodation (Bannister House) or (Jessica Dunne Lodge).

• Our Mobile Support service offers a range of services for young people to secure and sustain stable accommodation.
